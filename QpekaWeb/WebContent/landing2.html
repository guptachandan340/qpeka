What is Qpeka

Qpeka is an awesome new free reading platform, where readers can get books, short stories, poems, articles, blogs all for free. We have an author-oriented approach to publishing on our platform which makes it possible for them to earn even though its free to the readers. New as well as established authors and publishers can contact us to know more.

The dynamics of our platform are the stepping stone to help us reach our goal to make the LARGEST ONLINE READING PLATFORM in the world. Do make sure to go through our concepts and take advantage of our offers for early registration!

Qpeka for Reader

Whatever you read at Qpeka is available for free. What's more, your reading progress is automatically logged by us. So, the next time you login you resume where you had left off earlier. We have an amazing reward points system as well, where readers get points for their involvement with the website. They can redeem the points for free gifts, free services etc. Further, we also have a level system, by which your level determines how many points you get. Qpeka also functions as a social interaction platform for readers and writers to share their views and connect with their friends. Thus, you can talk about your favorite reads, see your friends activities and recommend them something for reading etc. We have loads of other interesting features coming as well including mobile applications for support on various portable devices, to ensure you have Qpeka when you want wherever you want.

Qpeka for Writers

If you are an author, Qpeka is a perfect publishing platform for you. Along with the highest reader exposure on account of the free reading principle, Qpeka will help you earn for every page of your work as soon as it read by someone. Thus effectively, you earn royalty for every page read. Further, your level shall determine the rate of royalty per page. We also help author-publisher relations, by taking across promising content to publishers/ producers etc to be recreated in other artistic forms. A robust review system also provides the you and the readers the necessary inputs for the works. We also provide a host of other writer oriented services such as copyrighting, editing, cover designing, etc. to ensure that you can improve the quality of the works. All in all, we at Qpeka are committed to give the best to our authors' community.
